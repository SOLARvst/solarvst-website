About SOLAR VST

Measured in circuits.

SOLAR VST is a boutique plugin studio dedicated to one thing: recreating the exact electronic behavior of vintage hardware from the 1970s and 1980s, with no shortcuts.

Every plugin begins with the original schematics. We trace the signal path component by component, model the nonlinearities, the thermal drift, the harmonic character, until the digital version is indistinguishable from the real machine.

We ship when it's right, not when it's fast. Small team. High standards. No compromises.

Acid Synthesizer

SOLAR 303

A meticulous transistor-level recreation of the Roland TB-303 Bass Line. The filter, the accent, the slide, modeled from the original 1981 circuitry to capture every characteristic of the machine that defined acid house.

  • Sawtooth & Square oscillators
  • 16-step sequencer with MIDI export
  • Reverb · Delay · Distortion
  • Full MIDI CC Learn
  • 4 visual skin themes

Filter Effect

SOLAR LADDER

A faithful emulation of the Moog transistor ladder filter, one of the most revered circuits in synthesis history. Four cascaded transistor stages modeled with distributed nonlinearities for authentic warmth, resonance, and self-oscillation.

  • Moog-style 4-pole & 2-pole modes
  • HP / LP topology switching
  • Stereo & Mono envelope follower
  • Drive with harmonic saturation
  • Full MIDI CC Learn
